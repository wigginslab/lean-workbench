Innovation Accounting

The Lean methodology defines its own accounting -- Innovation Accounting -- with separate analyses appropriate to different points in the life-cycle of a business hypothesis: baseline quantification, optimization, and pivoting.

Baseline Quantification

In baseline quantification, we test your minimum viable product (MVP) and quantify the parameters on which your business projections rest.
