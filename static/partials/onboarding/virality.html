Virality Engine

The virality engine thrives on getting the word out about your product. Who's talking about your work and why? The key performance indicator is viral coefficient,the number of users each user brings on, and this can be measured using the Facebook and Twitter APIs. We estimate the viral coefficient with a formula that weighs product mentions and interactions with your product pages by the influence of the user interacting.
